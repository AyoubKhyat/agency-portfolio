## Why SEO Is Crucial in Marrakech

Marrakech is a city where competition is fierce — restaurants, riads, real estate agencies, craft shops... Thousands of businesses compete for the attention of the same customers. The difference between a business that thrives and one that stagnates? **Its visibility on Google.**

When a tourist searches "restaurant Gueliz" or a resident types "plumber Marrakech", the top 3 results capture **75% of clicks**. If you're not there, your competitors are getting your potential customers.

## The Basics of Local SEO

### 1. Google Business Profile: Your Top Priority

If you only do one thing, do this. Your Google Business Profile (formerly Google My Business) is the first thing people see when they search for your type of business.

For an optimized listing:

- **Fill in all information**: address, hours, phone, website
- **Choose the right categories**: be specific (not just "restaurant" but "traditional Moroccan restaurant")
- **Add quality photos**: at least 10 photos of your establishment, products, and team
- **Collect reviews**: systematically ask satisfied customers to leave a Google review
- **Respond to all reviews**: positive and negative, always professionally

### 2. Local Keywords

The key to local SEO is targeting the right queries. Here's how to find yours:

- **[Your service] + Marrakech**: "dentist Marrakech", "wedding photographer Marrakech"
- **[Your service] + neighborhood**: "hairdresser Gueliz", "cafe Hivernage"
- **Frequent questions**: "best [service] in Marrakech", "where to find [product] Marrakech"

Integrate these keywords naturally in:
- Your page titles
- Descriptions
- H1, H2, H3 headings
- Your site's text
- Image alt tags

### 3. A Technically Optimized Website

Google evaluates the technical quality of your site. The essential criteria:

- **Loading speed**: your site must load in less than 3 seconds
- **Mobile-first**: the mobile display must be flawless
- **HTTPS**: SSL certificate is mandatory
- **Structured markup**: schema markup helps Google understand your content
- **Core Web Vitals**: LCP, FID, CLS — Google's performance metrics

## Advanced Strategies

### Content Is King

Google loves fresh and useful content. A company blog is your best SEO ally. Write articles that answer your customers' questions:

- A restaurant can publish Moroccan recipes
- A riad can write about the best activities in Marrakech
- An artisan can tell the story of their craft

**The golden rule**: one blog post per month minimum. Each article is a new gateway to your site.

### Local Backlinks

Incoming links (backlinks) remain a major ranking factor. For a Marrakech business:

- Register in **local directories** (Pages Jaunes Maroc, Maroc Annonces)
- Participate in **local events** and get mentions on organizers' websites
- Collaborate with local **bloggers and influencers**
- Join **professional associations** in your sector

### Social Media as an SEO Lever

Although social signals are not a direct ranking factor, they indirectly boost your SEO:

- More shares = more visibility = more natural backlinks
- Your Instagram or Facebook profile also appears in Google results
- Reviews on social media strengthen your credibility

## Common SEO Mistakes in Marrakech

1. **Keyword stuffing** — Repeating "Marrakech" 50 times on a page won't help you, quite the opposite
2. **Ignoring Arabic content** — The Arabic-speaking market is huge, think bilingual
3. **Unoptimized images** — 5 MB photos slow down your site and penalize your SEO
4. **No mobile strategy** — Over 70% of searches are done on mobile in Morocco
5. **Impatience** — SEO takes 3-6 months to deliver results. It's a marathon, not a sprint.

## Measuring Your Results

Track these metrics to evaluate your progress:

- **Google Search Console**: positions, clicks, impressions (free)
- **Google Analytics**: traffic, visitor behavior (free)
- **Local ranking**: regularly check your position on your target keywords
